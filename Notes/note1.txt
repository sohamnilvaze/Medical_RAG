Metoprolol 50 mg twice daily to maintain heart rate < 80 bpm. Follow-up in cardiology clinic in two weeks. Lifestyle modification including low-sodium diet, regular aerobic exercise, and weight loss plan advised. Education on warning signs such as syncope, chest pain, or recurrent palpitations provided. She was discharged in normal sinus rhythm, asymptomatic, and afebrile.
